# DYNASTY FORMAT QUICK REFERENCE - ALL VARIANTS

### LEAGUE SIZE

8-TEAM: shallow. Top 50 WRs are starters.
10-TEAM: standard "casual" dynasty. ~70 WRs starters.
12-TEAM: gold standard. ~85 WRs starters.
14-TEAM: deep. ~100 WRs starters.
16-TEAM: deepest standard. Bench every spot meaningful.

### ROSTER SIZE

22-23: tight; aggressive churn.
25-27: standard.
28-30 + taxi: full dynasty experience.
30+ + taxi + IR: deep dynasty.

### QB FORMAT

1QB: single QB starter; QB12 is replacement-level.
SUPERFLEX: WR/RB/TE/QB flex; effectively 2 QB starters; QB24 is replacement.
2QB: two dedicated QB slots; same as SF effectively.

### PPR FORMAT

0 PPR: standard non-PPR; rare in modern dynasty.
0.5 PPR: half-PPR; balanced format.
1.0 PPR: full PPR; most common; inflates pass-catchers.

### TE FORMAT

STANDARD: 1.0 PPR for TE.
TIGHT END PREMIUM (TEP): 1.5 PPR for TE OR 0.5 bonus per TE catch.
HEAVY TEP: 2.0 PPR for TE (rare).

### DEFENSE FORMAT

DST: team defense unit. Standard.
IDP BASIC: 4-6 individual defensive starters.
IDP DEEP: 8-15 defensive starters.

### LEAGUE TYPE

DYNASTY: full roster carries.
KEEPER: N players carry, rest reset annually.
DEVY: includes college player rights.
CONTRACT/SALARY CAP: includes contract lengths and cap.

### PLATFORM

SLEEPER: free, mobile-first; modern dynasty default.
MFL (MyFantasyLeague): paid; veteran dynasty platform.
ESPN: limited dynasty support.
YAHOO: limited dynasty support.
FFPC: paid premium; SF + TEP standard.
FLEAFLICKER: free; supports IDP well.
FANTRAX: paid; salary cap support.
REALITY SPORTS ONLINE: salary cap dynasty.

### PICK VALUE ADJUSTMENTS

10-TEAM 1QB: pick values BASELINE -10%.
12-TEAM 1QB: BASELINE.
14-TEAM 1QB: BASELINE +10%.
12-TEAM SF: BASELINE +20%.
12-TEAM SF + TEP: BASELINE +25%.

### DECISION GUIDE: WHICH FORMAT TO RUN

NEW LEAGUE / CASUAL OWNERS:
- 12-team
- 1QB
- 0.5 or 1.0 PPR
- 25-roster
- DST (no IDP)
- No taxi

INTERMEDIATE LEAGUE:
- 12-team
- SF
- 1.0 PPR
- 27-roster + 4 taxi
- DST
- Trade 2 future picks

ADVANCED LEAGUE:
- 12-team
- SF + TEP
- 1.0 PPR
- 30-roster + 6 taxi + 4 IR
- IDP basic
- Trade 3 future picks
- Annual orphan auction protocol

ULTRA LEAGUE:
- 12-team
- SF + TEP + Salary Cap
- 1.0 PPR
- 35-roster + 8 taxi
- Full IDP
- 5-year contracts
- Devy

### ROOKIE DRAFT FORMAT

3 ROUNDS: minimum standard.
4 ROUNDS: most leagues.
5+ ROUNDS: deep dynasty.

### DRAFT TYPE
- LINEAR: 1.01-1.12, 2.01-2.12 (no reverse).
- SNAKE: standard reverse-snake.
- 3RD ROUND REVERSAL (3RR): rounds 1-2 normal, round 3+ reversed.

### OFFSEASON CALENDAR FOR DYNASTY

JAN: post-championship audit.
FEB: combine, deep prospect dive.
MAR: NFL Free Agency (huge value shift).
APR: NFL Draft (largest single-event reprice).
MAY: rookie drafts.
JUN: market settle / fine-tune.
JUL: pre-camp positioning.
AUG: training camp + preseason.
SEP-NOV: regular season + trade deadline (Wks 9-12).
DEC: playoffs.
