DYNASTY OFFSEASON CALENDAR - MONTH BY MONTH PLAYBOOK
=====================================================
Sources synthesized: Dynasty Nerds monthly calendar series (May, June), Footballguys "Decoding the Dynasty Calendar," Dynasty Deal Maker calendar, Fantasy Life "What's Next After a Successful Season," NFL annual cycle.

THE 12-MONTH CYCLE
------------------
Dynasty isn't a 17-week game; it's a 365-day game. The owners who systematically beat their leagues do so because they outwork others between February and August.

JANUARY - POST-CHAMPIONSHIP AUDIT
- WHAT'S HAPPENING IN NFL: regular season ending, playoffs starting.
- WHAT TO DO: Conduct an honest team-value audit. What did your team look like at peak vs at end? What aged out? What ascended?
- TRADE TARGETS: contenders' aging vets (their owners are riding the wave; you might pry one cheap if their playoff run ended).
- PICKS: future picks at low offseason prices. BUY 2027 1sts and 2028 picks if you're a rebuilder.
- KEY ACTIVITY: write down your phase decision (contender / reload / rebuild) for the year.

FEBRUARY - NFL COMBINE / RECON SEASON
- WHAT'S HAPPENING IN NFL: combine, senior bowl already done. Most managers haven't researched rookies yet.
- WHAT TO DO: Deep dive on incoming rookie class. Tier-rank YOUR way before consensus solidifies.
- TRADE TARGETS: managers selling rookie pick options before they know what they have.
- PICKS: rookie picks generally low value vs March/April. BUY mid-1sts.
- INSIGHT: most managers are dormant. This is your edge window.

MARCH - NFL FREE AGENCY (HUGE EVENT)
- WHAT'S HAPPENING IN NFL: legal tampering ~ March 11. Free agency opens ~ March 13. Cap cuts.
- WHAT TO DO: REALLOCATE values massively. Every RB, WR, QB on a new team has a new value. Be among the first to react.
- KEY MOVES:
  * BUY players who land in upgraded offenses (better OL, better QB, better target share).
  * SELL players whose teams added competition.
  * SELL players staying on bad teams whose contracts force them to be there.
- TRADE VOLUME: spikes 2-3x in March. More opportunities to extract value from misaligned valuations.

APRIL - NFL DRAFT (LARGEST SINGLE-EVENT REPRICE)
- WHAT'S HAPPENING IN NFL: NFL Draft late April.
- WHAT TO DO PRE-DRAFT: trade rookie picks at peak inflated value if you don't want to use them.
- WHAT TO DO POST-DRAFT: massive role/value reshuffles. Players who got cannibalized by rookies = sell or accept devaluation.
- ROOKIE TARGETS: identify rookies in great landing spots BEFORE rookie draft slots set.
- PICK TRADING: pick value PEAKS in April (often 120%+ of offseason baseline).

MAY - ROOKIE DRAFT MONTH
- WHAT'S HAPPENING IN DYNASTY: rookie drafts run May 1-15 typically.
- WHAT TO DO: stick to your tier sheet. Don't reach. Trade-back if you have multiple options at same tier.
- POST-DRAFT: keep an eye on rookies you DIDN'T draft. Year 1 trades are often very profitable on rookies who slip in your league but rise nationally.
- TRADE TARGETS: managers who reached early and want to "fix" their boards.
- INSIGHT: this is the second-best window of the year for negotiation. Owners are most engaged.

JUNE - MARKET CORRECTION / FINE-TUNING
- WHAT'S HAPPENING IN NFL: OTAs, mini-camps. Beat reporters start describing camp battles.
- WHAT TO DO: minor roster tuning. Identify under-radar position battles you think YOUR guy wins.
- TRADE TARGETS: small swaps to fix a single hole, not big trades.
- AVOID: overpaying for rookie hype that's still rising.
- INSIGHT: market activity drops 50-70% from May. Patience pays.

JULY - PRE-CAMP POSITIONING
- WHAT'S HAPPENING IN NFL: vet contracts done, training camps imminent.
- WHAT TO DO: position for camp battles you've identified. Buy potential winners; sell potential losers.
- TRADE TARGETS: managers who haven't paid attention since May. They're often willing to reset.
- KEY ACTIVITY: review schedule. Identify which of your players have great Week 14-17 schedules.

AUGUST - CAMP NARRATIVE / PRESEASON
- WHAT'S HAPPENING IN NFL: training camps, then preseason games.
- WHAT TO DO: BUY INJURY PANIC - many "injuries" are minor. SELL CAMP HYPE - 80% of "Camp Stars" don't translate.
- INSIGHT: your final offseason buy/sell window. Activity spikes again as draft week looms.

SEPTEMBER - SEASON START
- WHAT'S HAPPENING IN NFL: regular season begins.
- WHAT TO DO: lineup decisions. Set early baselines.
- TRADE TARGETS: nervous owners post-Week 1 panic. Some are trade-ready by Week 3 if their team starts 0-3.
- INSIGHT: sample-size discipline matters. Don't overreact to single games.

OCTOBER - EARLY-SEASON TRADE WINDOW
- WHAT'S HAPPENING IN NFL: Week 4-8.
- WHAT TO DO: by Week 5, your phase is clear. Act on it.
- BUYING: for contenders, buy productive WRs from rebuild teams.
- SELLING: for rebuilders, sell aging vets to contenders. Their playoff stress = your leverage.

NOVEMBER - TRADE DEADLINE LEVERAGE PEAK
- WHAT'S HAPPENING IN NFL: Week 9-12.
- WHAT TO DO: most leverage of the year. NFL trade deadline-aligned dynasty deadlines (typically Week 11) compress urgency.
- KEY ACTIVITY: contenders pay 1.5-2x premium for known production. Rebuilders accept that premium happily.
- TRADE TARGETS: aged-out vets to contender. Contender's bench WRs to rebuilder + a 2027 1st coming back.

DECEMBER - PLAYOFFS + OFFSEASON PREP
- WHAT'S HAPPENING IN NFL: regular season ends.
- WHAT TO DO: lineup hyperfocus. Don't lose championship by overthinking.
- ALSO: begin scouting next year's rookie class (CFP playoff games are draft-eligible showcase).
- IF ELIMINATED: start the rebuild assessment now. Don't wait until January.

KEY SEASONAL CADENCE PRINCIPLES
-------------------------------
1. Pick values rise from January (offseason baseline) to April (peak).
2. Aging vets values DROP from January to August (offseason aging tax).
3. Aging vet values RISE again from August to November (contender desperation premium).
4. Rookies' values are most volatile in May (post-draft) - up or down 50% based on landing spot.
5. Productive 26-and-under WRs are the steadiest asset class - little seasonal volatility.

TRADE COUNT BENCHMARKS BY WINNING OWNERS
----------------------------------------
- Bottom-quartile owners: 0-3 trades per year.
- Median: 4-7 trades per year.
- Top-quartile (winners): 10-20+ trades per year.
- Trade volume IS NOT the cause of winning, but high-volume traders are MORE likely to win because they generate more positive-EV opportunities.

THE 4 BIG TRADE WINDOWS (ANNUAL)
--------------------------------
1. February: dormant-market mispricing.
2. April: NFL Draft reprice frenzy.
3. May: rookie draft over-correction.
4. November: contender-deadline desperation.

If you only execute trades in these 4 windows, you'll outperform 80% of owners.

LEAGUE-CHAT MAINTENANCE
-----------------------
- Year-round soft engagement. Active leagues trade more.
- Drop "what's everyone thinking on X" type messages quarterly.
- Keep relationships warm; don't only message when you want a trade.
