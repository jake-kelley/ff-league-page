# DYNASTY FANTASY FOOTBALL GLOSSARY
Terms used in dynasty discourse, with definitions and context.

ADP - Average Draft Position. Where a player gets drafted on average across leagues.

AGE CLIFF - The age at which a player's production sharply drops. RB: ~27-28. WR: ~30. TE: ~31-32. QB: ~36-37.

ANCHOR RB - A drafting strategy that takes one elite RB early and waits on the rest. Subset of Hero RB.

ASSET - Any tradable resource: player or pick.

BACK-FILL - Adding waivers/trades to fill holes after a starter is hurt or moved.

BELL COW - An RB getting the majority of touches. Workhorse role.

BLUE CHIP - Top-tier elite player with proven production and long career window.

BOOM/BUST - High variance player. Big games and zero games.

BPA - Best Player Available (drafting strategy).

BUSTED PICK - A high pick that didn't return value.

BUY LOW - Acquiring a player at a temporarily depressed value.

C2C - Campus2Canton. Devy variant where college games count for scoring.

CALC - Trade calculator (KTC, FantasyCalc, DLF, etc.).

CHURN - Constant adding/dropping at end of bench.

COMMITTEE BACKFIELD - Multiple RBs sharing touches; reduces fantasy value of all.

CONSOLIDATION - Trading multiple pieces for one better player. 2-for-1, 3-for-1.

CONTENDER - Team with realistic championship odds in current/next season.

CONTRACT LEAGUE - Dynasty variant with player salaries and contract lengths.

CRYBABY - Owner who complains about every trade.

CUTTING - Releasing a player from your roster.

DEADCAP - Salary still counted against your cap after cutting a player.

DELAY GAME - Strategy of waiting to make moves until peak market timing.

DEPTH CHART - NFL team's hierarchy of players at each position.

DEVY - Developmental league. Drafting college players before NFL.

DOMINATOR RATING - Market share metric for college players.

DST - Defense / Special Teams (team unit).

DUMP TRUCK - A rebuilder who's unloading vets en masse.

DYNASTY - League where you keep your roster across multiple seasons.

EARLY 1ST - 1.01-1.04 rookie pick.

EDGE - Pass-rushing defensive lineman / outside LB.

EDP - Expected fantasy Draft Position.

FAAB - Free Agent Acquisition Budget. Bidding system for waivers.

FANTASY POINTS PER GAME (FPG) - Average production metric.

FLEX - Flexible starting position (any of WR/RB/TE typically).

FORWARD - Trading forward = shedding aging assets for picks.

GAAP - Generally Accepted Accounting Principles. Used metaphorically by DLF for "Generally Accepted Asset Principles" in dynasty.

GLUE GUY - Mid-tier reliable starter without star power.

HANDCUFF - Backup RB whose value depends on starter injury.

HERO RB - Strategy of taking one elite RB and waiting on others.

HOLD - Refusing to trade a player.

INJURED RESERVE (IR) - Roster slot for actually injured players.

K - Kicker.

KEEPER LEAGUE - League where N players carry over each year (vs full dynasty).

KTC - KeepTradeCut, a popular crowdsourced value site.

LATE 1ST - 1.09-1.12 rookie pick.

LIFETIME LEAGUE - League intended to run indefinitely.

LIQUIDITY - How easily an asset can be traded at fair value.

LOTTERY TICKET - Cheap stash with high upside potential.

MID 1ST - 1.05-1.08 rookie pick.

MORTALITY TABLE - Probability framework for player aging (Harstad).

NFL DRAFT CAPITAL - Where a player was drafted. Strongest predictor of fantasy success.

OPPORTUNITY COST - The value of the alternative not chosen.

ORPHAN - Dynasty team abandoned by previous owner; needs new manager.

OVERPAYING - Paying more than calc value, often justified for studs.

PASSIVE OWNER - Owner who rarely trades or makes moves.

PHASE - Contender / reload / rebuild stage.

PICK CAPITAL - Quantity and quality of picks owned.

PIVOT - Switching from one phase to another (contender to rebuilder).

POST-HYPE SLEEPER - Year 4-5 player who hasn't hit yet but might.

POWER RANKING - Site/tool ranking of dynasty roster strength.

PPR - Points Per Reception scoring. 1.0 = full PPR; 0.5 = half PPR.

PRE-HYPE - Player about to break out before market notices.

PREMIUM - Inflated price relative to baseline.

QB1 - Top-12 QB OR a starting QB on an NFL team.

REBUILD - Strategic teardown for future championship.

RECENCY BIAS - Overweighting recent performance.

REDRAFT - Single-season fantasy league.

RELOAD - Mid-pack team retooling for near-term contention.

RFA - Restricted Free Agent (some leagues use this).

ROBUST RB - Drafting many RBs early.

ROOKIE DRAFT - Annual draft of NFL rookies.

ROOKIE PICK - A draft slot in an upcoming rookie draft.

ROSTER CRUNCH - Too many wanted players, too few roster spots.

SELL HIGH - Trading at peak value.

SF / SUPERFLEX - Format with a flex slot that allows QB.

SHALLOW LEAGUE - 8-10 team league with shallow rosters.

SHORT WINDOW - Limited time to contend.

SHUTOUT - Bye week with no useful starters.

SLEEPER - Undervalued player with breakout potential. Also: the platform Sleeper.com.

SLOT WR - WR who plays primarily in the slot. Possession profile.

STARTUP DRAFT - Initial draft creating dynasty league.

STASH - Bench/taxi player held for future value.

STREAMER - Weekly waiver-wire pickup for one matchup.

STUD - Top-tier player at his position.

TAXI SQUAD - Extra roster slots for rookies that don't count against active limits.

TEP - Tight End Premium scoring.

TIER - Group of similarly-valued players.

TIER BREAK - Gap between tiers; signal to trade.

TILT - Emotional state after losses; bad time to trade.

TPRR - Targets Per Route Run.

TRADE BACK - Trading down for additional picks.

TRADE BLOCK - List of available players for trade.

TRADE UP - Trading multiple picks for higher slot.

TRADE WINDOW - Time period when trade volume peaks.

TROPHY HUNTING - Building solely for one championship year.

TURNED THE CORNER - Phase transition: rebuilder becoming contender.

UDFA - Undrafted Free Agent (NFL).

UNDERCUTTING - Bidding less than market for a player.

UPSIDE - Ceiling potential.

VEGA / VEGAS - Refers to Vegas betting lines for fantasy game-script projection.

VERDICT - Calculator's grade on a trade.

VET - Veteran player (4+ years in NFL).

WAIVER WIRE - Free agent pool.

WIN-NOW - Sacrificing future for immediate championship odds.

WORKHORSE - RB getting 80%+ of touches.

WR1 / WR2 / WR3 - Tier indicator (top-12, top-24, top-36).

YAC - Yards After Catch.

YPC - Yards Per Carry.

YPRR / Y/RR - Yards per Route Run. Top efficiency metric for WR.

ZERO RB - Strategy of skipping RB early in draft.

### DYNASTY-SPECIFIC PHRASES

"Buy the dip" - Acquire when price drops.
"Cut your losses" - Drop a stash that's not working.
"Don't bag-hold" - Don't keep an asset whose thesis broke.
"Don't fall in love" - Stay rational with your players.
"Future is brighter" - Argument for rebuild.
"Hit on the pick" - Drafted a player who became valuable.
"Lock him in" - Long-term commitment to a player.
"Move while you can" - Sell before value declines.
"Roster spot is currency" - Every spot has opportunity cost.
"Studs win trades" - Mantra of "always get the best player."
"Talent endures, situation evaporates" - Buy talent over situation.
"Trade often, trade fair" - Active trading philosophy.
"You can't win a championship in February" - Don't overweight offseason moves.
"You don't need 10 RBs" - Push back against position hoarding.

### SPECIFIC PLATFORM TERMS

SLEEPER:
- Continuous waivers - run automatically on FA add.
- Trade block - in-app trade market.
- League chat - in-app messaging.

MFL (MyFantasyLeague):
- Conferences - multi-league merged tournaments.
- Salary - salary cap support native.

ESPN:
- Limited dynasty support; many leagues moved to Sleeper/MFL.

YAHOO:
- Limited dynasty native; some leagues operate manually.

FFPC:
- Premier dynasty paid league host.
- Unique scoring (TEP standard, etc.).

FLEAFLICKER:
- Free; supports dynasty + IDP well.

FANTRAX:
- Paid; complex commissioner tools.
- Strong for salary cap leagues.
