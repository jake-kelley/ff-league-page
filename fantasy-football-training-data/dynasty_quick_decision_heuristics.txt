DYNASTY QUICK DECISION HEURISTICS - "RULES OF THUMB" FOR FAST CALLS
====================================================================
Crystallized rules from dozens of dynasty sources. Use when you don't have time for a full analysis.

ROSTER MANAGEMENT HEURISTICS

H1: NEVER START THE SEASON WITH FEWER THAN 2 ROOKIE PICKS PER UPCOMING DRAFT.

H2: NEVER OWN MORE THAN 3 RBS AGE 27+.

H3: ALWAYS HAVE 1-2 ASCENDING (24-AND-UNDER) ANCHORS AT WR.

H4: IN SF, ALWAYS HAVE 3 STARTABLE QBs.

H5: DON'T HOARD HANDCUFFS WITHOUT THE LEAD.

H6: AT MOST 2 TES; PREFER 1 STUD OR 2 LOTTERY YOUNG.

H7: DON'T DRAFT KICKER OR DST BEFORE THE LAST 2 ROUNDS.

TRADE HEURISTICS

T1: STUDS WIN TRADES. Choose the side getting the best single asset 75% of the time.

T2: TRADE 3-FOR-1 TO ELITE > 2-FOR-1 TO ELITE > 1-FOR-1 LATERAL.

T3: SELL OLD VETS BEFORE 28TH BIRTHDAY (RB) OR 30TH (WR).

T4: BUY YOUNG WR WITH 25%+ TARGET SHARE EVEN IF COUNTING STATS LACK.

T5: 30-DAY TREND >+15% = SELL WINDOW; <-15% = BUY WINDOW.

T6: NEVER REJECT WITHOUT A COUNTER (unless wildly off-market).

T7: NEVER ACCEPT FIRST OFFER (always counter for at least minor improvement).

T8: TRADE WITH CONTENDERS ON SUNDAY EVENINGS WHEN THEY LOST.

T9: TRADE WITH REBUILDERS IN JANUARY-FEBRUARY WHEN THEY'RE PRICED FOR DUMPING.

T10: WHEN IN DOUBT, OFFER MULTIPLE OPTIONS AND LET THE OPPONENT CHOOSE.

ROOKIE DRAFT HEURISTICS

R1: TRUST NFL DRAFT CAPITAL ABOVE COLLEGE PRODUCTION.

R2: 1.01 IN SF IS USUALLY THE TOP QB IF ONE EXISTS.

R3: 1.01 IN 1QB IS USUALLY THE TOP RB OR WR.

R4: TIER-BASED, NOT SLOT-BASED. Multiple options at same tier = trade back.

R5: DON'T CHASE LANDING SPOTS - TALENT ENDURES.

R6: AVOID DRAFTING HANDCUFFS IN ROOKIE DRAFTS.

R7: DON'T DRAFT QBS EARLY IN 1QB.

R8: ALWAYS HAVE A 3RD-ROUND DART THROW PLAN.

R9: DON'T REACH ON HYPE - STICK TO YOUR TIER SHEET.

PHASE HEURISTICS

P1: NEVER STAY IN THE MIDDLE. Choose contender or rebuilder each offseason.

P2: A REBUILD IS 2-YEAR MINIMUM. Don't pivot back too soon.

P3: CONTENDERS PAY PREMIUMS FOR PRODUCTION; REBUILDERS PAY PREMIUMS FOR PICKS.

P4: BY WEEK 5, YOUR PHASE IS CLEAR. Act on it.

P5: AT TRADE DEADLINE (WEEK 11-12), CONTENDERS HAVE THEIR LAST CHANCE TO BUY.

LINEUP HEURISTICS

L1: STARTUDS EVERY WEEK. Don't matchup-fade top-12.

L2: ALWAYS START YOUR ROOKIE OVER A LOW-CEILING VET IF OUT OF CONTENTION.

L3: USE IR SLOTS - DON'T LEAVE THEM EMPTY.

L4: SET LINEUPS BY FRIDAY (some platforms lock Sunday morning).

L5: STREAM K, DST, AND QB2 BY MATCHUP IF NEEDED.

L6: AVOID STREAMING IN PLAYOFFS - NEED KNOWN FLOORS.

WAIVER HEURISTICS

W1: DON'T BLOW MORE THAN 40% OF FAAB ON ANY SINGLE PLAYER unless season-changing.

W2: BUY ROLE EXPANSIONS, NOT ONE-WEEK SPIKES.

W3: USE TAXI FOR ROOKIES. Don't burn active spots.

W4: SAVE 15-20% OF FAAB FOR LATE-SEASON STRIKES.

VALUE HEURISTICS

V1: TRIANGULATE ACROSS KTC + FANTASYCALC + YOUR JUDGMENT.

V2: CALC DOESN'T KNOW YOUR LEAGUE. Adjust for scoring/depth/format.

V3: 2 YEARS FORWARD: PROJECT EVERY ASSET.

V4: AGE +2: imagine your player 2 years from now. Will he still start?

V5: LIQUIDITY > MARGINAL CALC VALUE. Tradable > stuck.

NEGOTIATION HEURISTICS

N1: ASK BEFORE OFFERING.

N2: NEVER BLAME OPPONENT'S COUNTER. Just engage or walk.

N3: SHOW CALC NUMBERS. Backed-up offers accepted 40%+ more.

N4: REPUTATION COMPOUNDS. Be hard but fair.

N5: WALK AWAY ATTITUDE > DESPERATION.

WHEN-IN-DOUBT HEURISTICS

D1: TAKE THE BPA when stuck on rookie draft choice.

D2: SELL when in doubt about an aging vet.

D3: HOLD young players (24-and-under) when in doubt.

D4: TRADE when in doubt about long-term value.

D5: ASK A NEUTRAL THIRD PARTY (Reddit, podcaster) when stuck.

LEAGUE COMMUNICATION HEURISTICS

C1: DON'T SHIT-TALK ABOUT TRADES PUBLICLY.

C2: DON'T BRAG ABOUT YOUR WINS PUBLICLY.

C3: ANSWER ALL TRADE OFFERS WITHIN 24 HOURS (yes, no, or counter).

C4: DON'T VETO unless clear collusion.

C5: BE THE OWNER OTHERS WANT TO TRADE WITH.

EMERGENCY HEURISTICS

E1: STARTER GOES IR -> immediate FAAB action.

E2: NFL TRADE/RELEASE -> reprice all your assets affected.

E3: COACH FIRED MID-SEASON -> reassess offensive players.

E4: YOUR TEAM 0-3 -> begin rebuild conversations Week 4-5.

E5: YOUR TEAM 5-1 -> begin contender consolidation Week 6-7.

GENERAL DYNASTY MANTRAS

M1: BUY LOW, SELL HIGH. (Always.)

M2: TALENT ENDURES, SITUATION EVAPORATES.

M3: DRAFT PICKS ARE DEAR.

M4: TRADE OFTEN, TRADE FAIR.

M5: PROCESS OVER RESULTS.

M6: DYNASTY IS A MARATHON, NOT A SPRINT.

M7: DON'T GET STUCK IN THE MIDDLE.

M8: THE NEXT TRADE IS MORE IMPORTANT THAN THE LAST.

M9: NEVER FALL IN LOVE.

M10: AGE > PRODUCTION.

THE CRYSTAL HEURISTIC
---------------------
If you remember nothing else from this entire training corpus:

"Identify your phase, sell aging vets to contenders, buy young WRs with target share, never pay a 1st for a 28+ RB, and trade more often than you currently do."

That single sentence captures 70%+ of dynasty alpha generation.
