DYNASTY ANALYST PERSPECTIVES - AGGREGATED INSIGHTS
====================================================
Sources synthesized from: FantasyPros (Pat Fitzmaurice, Andrew Erickson, Derek Brown), Dynasty League Football, Dynasty Nerds Podcast (Garret Price + crew), Footballguys (Maurile Tremblay, Sigmund Bloom, Jeff Bell, Ryan Hester), Hayden Winks + Josh Norris (Underdog), PFF (Nathan Jahnke), Sharp Football (Rich Hribar), Mike Clay (ESPN), Justin Boone (Yahoo), RotoWire dynasty crew, RotoBaller, Fantasy Footballers (Kyle Borgognoni, Andy Holloway, Mike Wright).

THIS FILE: distilled "consensus" perspectives plus distinct individual takes from major dynasty analysts.

CONSENSUS POINTS (most analysts agree)

1. WR-CENTRIC DYNASTY ROSTER CONSTRUCTION
- Modern roster building emphasizes WR depth.
- WRs hold value longest; the asset class with the best aging curve.
- Aim for 8-10+ WRs on roster.

2. RB DEPRECIATION REALITY
- RBs decline fast (age 27-28).
- Treat as depreciating commodity.
- Hero RB / Anchor RB beats Robust RB strategically.

3. NFL DRAFT CAPITAL > LANDING SPOT > PRODUCTION (for rookies)
- Almost universal: NFL draft pick is the strongest single signal.
- Talent endures; situations evaporate.
- Don't over-correct on landing spots.

4. PICKS ARE CURRENCY
- The medium of dynasty trade is rookie picks.
- Future picks are systematically undervalued.
- Maintain a stockpile.

5. CONTENDER-OR-REBUILDER, NEVER MIDDLE
- Pick a phase each offseason.
- The middle is where teams die.

6. AGE > PRODUCTION FOR DYNASTY VALUE
- A 23 yo with 850 yards > a 28 yo with 1,100 yards in dynasty.
- Counts stats are WHO he was; age tells you WHO HE'LL BE.

7. SUPERFLEX REWIRES VALUE
- QB premium in SF is real.
- Don't apply 1QB rankings to SF rosters.

8. TIGHT END PREMIUM CHANGES STRATEGY
- Elite TEs become top-30 overall in TEP.
- Pay up for elite young TE in TEP.

WHERE ANALYSTS DISAGREE (DEBATES)

ZERO RB vs HERO RB:
- ZERO RB: more dynasty-friendly long-term.
- HERO RB: balances current and future production.

EARLY QB IN 1QB:
- PRO: lock in long-term scoring.
- CON: better value at QB in mid-rounds.

VETERAN VALUE PURCHASES:
- PRO: undervalued, immediate production.
- CON: aging into nothing fast.

ROOKIE PICK PREMIUM:
- PRO: optionality, future class strength.
- CON: hit rate is overstated; vets are guaranteed.

SUPERFLEX EXPANSION:
- PRO: rewards QB scarcity, rewires dynasty correctly.
- CON: makes mid-tier QBs over-valued.
